DRAGONSLAYERS CYCLE LOST BUT FOUND

The artistic expression of Lukáš Cinkanič is not only transferred to the canvas, but also expands into public space. In his exhibition Lost but Found, held simultaneously at Gallery Mimochodem and Billboart Gallery, he complements his painting with small objects that he transforms from found waste materials. He visually directs and incorporates these objects into newly emerging site-specific works. The artifacts, which Lukáš approaches with a certain ceremony, and which can function as ritual objects, weapons or horcruxes, become the residue of the artist's process of creation in the dystopian atmosphere of a post-pandemic world.
